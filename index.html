About me

I am a researcher in machine learning for Earth observation. Currently, I'm working on wildfire simulation as a postdoctoral research fellow at CNES in collaboration with the SPE lab from Corsica University and MLIA lab from Sorbonne University. My research focuses on hybrid modeling (the combination of physical models with machine learning models) and semi/self-supervised learning.

I completed my PhD at ONERA, Magellium and ISAE-SUPAERO in collaboration with the Toulouse Mathematics Institute on the semantic segmentation of airborne hyperspectral images for mapping impermeable surfaces in large urban areas.
