About me

I am a PhD candidate in machine learning for earth observation at ONERA, Magellium and ISAE-SUPAERO.

More specifically, I am developing algorithms to map impervious surfaces from airborne hyperspectral and spaceborne multispectral images.

I am supervised by Véronique Achard from ONERA, Laurent Risser from Toulouse Mathematics Institute, Béatrice Berthelot from Magellium and Xavier Briottet from ONERA.
